# A1 Installation Tutorial

This guide provides instructions on how to disassemble and reassemble the A1 printer. For detailed instructions on specific components, refer to the corresponding guide pages.

# A1 Disassembly

### Step 1- Remove the Y-axis Top Cover

#### Step 1:
1. Stand the printer upright.
2. Raise the X-axis to the top position.
3. Push the heatbed forward.
4. Remove the Y-axis Top Cover.

### Step 2- Disconnect the cables

#### Step 2:
1. Tilt the printer to expose the cable box on the bottom.
2. Disconnect the camera cable, X motor cable, and Z motor cable in sequence.
3. Loosen a silver screw and slide the cable box to disconnect the USB cable.
4. Open the Z-motor cable cover and remove the Z-motor cable.

### Step 3- Remove the Bottom Base Assembly

#### Step 3:
1. Move the heatbed forward.
2. Use an **H2.0 Allen Key** to remove 10 screws.
3. Move the heatbed backward.
4. Use an **H2.0 Allen Key** to remove 2 more screws.
5. Lift one side of the bottom base assembly first, then remove the entire assembly.

### Step 4- Remove the X-axis Assembly

#### Step 4:
1. Remove the 6 screws located at the back of the X-axis.
2. Detach the X-axis assembly from the printer frame.

# A1 Assembly

### Step 1- Install the X-axis Assembly

#### Step 1:
1. Align the X-axis with the locating pins on the two Z-axis sliders.
2. Install it onto the Z-axis sliders.
3. Secure the X-axis assembly by tightening 3 screws on each side.

### Step 2- Install the Bottom Base Assembly

#### Step 2:
1. Keep the Printer Frame upright.
2. Carefully install the bottom base assembly onto it, ensuring to avoid the Z-motor cable.
3. Move the heatbed forward to its furthest position and securely fasten 10 screws.
4. Move the heatbed backward to its furthest position and tighten 2 screws.

### Step 3- Connect the cables

#### Step 3:
1. Place the printer in a backward position.
2. Connect the Z-motor cable to the cable management box.
3. Install the cable management box onto the bottom base, slide it along the slot direction, connect the USB cable, and tighten one silver screw.
4. Follow the text prompts and color labels to connect the X motor cable, Z motor cable, and camera cable.
5. Organize the cables properly to ensure they are not being pressed or tangled.

### Step 4- Install the Y-axis Top Cover

#### Step 4:
1. Place the printer upright and move the heatbed forward to its furthest position.
2. Take the Y-axis Top Cover and pass it over through the bottom of the heatbed.
3. Push the cover forward until it reaches the end, ensuring that the cover aligns with the clips.
4. Secure the cover onto the clips.

**Special note:** If the heatbed is not moved all the way to the end, the Y-axis Top Cover may get stuck in the wrong position. In such a case, you can manually lift the Y-axis cover slightly to assist in its installation.

## Verify the Functionality

Ensure that the printing is smooth and free from any shaking. Double-check the installation of the bottom cable box and the arrangement of the cables.

## Calibration

It is recommended to perform a full calibration process before initiating any printing tasks.
